STORY

I know you crave to learn about the story, which is probably why you are here, but unfortunately, I won’t. “Why?” You may ask. It’s simple: the story has five different endings and progresses according to your choices. It all depends on which of the two main characters of our story suits you the best. Cain is always with Izist, for he is the soul inside him. While Izist, who has lived with Caine since birth, tries to decide whether to give him control of his life, nothing will be sure until you see all the endings of this adventure in which you control them!
